# Focus Sound - Privacy Policy

  **Last updated: May 2026**

  ## Overview
  Focus Sound does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

  ## Microphone Access
  Focus Sound requests microphone access solely to record audio directly into sound pads within
   the app. Recorded audio is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted
  externally.

  ## Audio Files
  Audio files you add to the app are accessed locally on your device. They are not uploaded or
  shared.

  ## Data Storage
  All app settings and project files are stored locally on your device using macOS standard
  storage (UserDefaults and local files).

  ## Third-Party Services
  Focus Sound does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

  ## Contact
  For questions or support, please open an issue in this repository.
